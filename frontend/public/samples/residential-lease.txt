FICTIONAL RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

Portfolio example only. The parties, address and terms below are fictional. This document is not legal advice.

This Residential Lease Agreement is entered into on 10 July 2026 between Kavita Nair (“Landlord”) and Rohan Das (“Tenant”) for Apartment 4B, Lakeview Residency, Bengaluru (“Premises”).

1. TERM
The lease begins on 1 August 2026 and continues for twelve months through 31 July 2027. Continued occupation after the term requires the Landlord’s written approval.

2. RENT
Monthly rent is INR 28,000 and must be paid on or before the fifth day of each month by bank transfer. Any change to the payment account must be confirmed in writing. The lease does not specify a late fee or grace period beyond the fifth day.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant will pay a refundable security deposit equal to two months’ rent. Landlord may deduct amounts for unpaid rent, unpaid charges, missing items and damage beyond ordinary wear. The lease does not state a refund deadline, inspection procedure, evidence standard, or deadline for an itemized deduction statement.

4. USE AND OCCUPANCY
The Premises may be used only as a private residence by Tenant and approved occupants. Illegal activity, nuisance and commercial use are prohibited. Guests staying more than fourteen consecutive days require written approval.

5. UTILITIES
Tenant will pay all utility and service charges relating to the Premises, including electricity, water, internet and cooking gas. The clause does not allocate shared-building charges, meter errors, or unpaid balances incurred before possession.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Tenant will keep the Premises reasonably clean and is responsible for minor repairs and routine maintenance. Landlord is responsible for structural repairs and major building systems not damaged by Tenant. “Minor repair” is not defined by category or monetary limit.

7. CONDITION REPORT
The parties should record the condition of the Premises, furniture, appliances, keys and meters at handover. No signed move-in inventory or photographic schedule is attached to this lease.

8. LANDLORD ACCESS
Landlord may enter the Premises at reasonable times to inspect, repair, show the property or comply with law. Except in emergencies, the lease does not require a minimum advance-notice period or restrict frequency.

9. ALTERATIONS AND PETS
Tenant may not make structural alterations, change locks, repaint, or install fixtures without written consent. Pets require prior written approval. Consent conditions and restoration standards should be documented separately.

10. EARLY TERMINATION
Tenant may terminate before the end of the term by giving written notice and paying an early-termination charge equal to two months’ rent. No exception is stated for uninhabitable conditions, Landlord breach, relocation, or replacement by an approved tenant.

11. DEFAULT
Failure to pay rent or material breach permits the non-breaching party to issue written notice and exercise remedies available by law. The lease does not specify a uniform cure period for non-payment and other breaches.

12. RENEWAL
The lease may be renewed on terms mutually agreed before expiration. It does not specify a renewal notice window, revised-rent formula, or automatic extension.

13. NOTICES
Notices must be delivered by email and registered post to the addresses provided by the parties. Each party must promptly notify the other of updated contact information.

14. GOVERNING LAW
This lease is governed by applicable Indian law. Courts in Bengaluru will have jurisdiction, subject to any mandatory tenancy procedure.
